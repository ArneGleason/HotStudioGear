My wife said it was "the console or the couch." Turns out that the floor is perfectly comfortable when you're absorbing the pure, unadulterated electrical hum of a 1974 analog mixing desk.

Look, does it consume more power than a small neighborhood? Yes. Do half the channels output a steady layer of white noise? Also yes. But when I adjust that one giant metal knob on the master bus, I swear my hearing aid picks up frequencies that digital audio just can't reproduce. 

This isn't about convenience. It's about suffering for the *töän*.
